My work: I aim to make language a complementary part of the interface for any application (e.g. from desktop spreadsheets to mobile apps). This involves solving a range of fascinating challenges through new methods in Artificial Intelligence and Crowdsourcing.

In mid-2022, I will start as a Senior Lecturer (ie., research tenure-track) at the University of Sydney. I have funding for PhD students and will be recruiting on the Northern and Southern Hemisphere cycles.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Michigan, working on Natural Language Processing and Crowdsourcing. I completed my Ph.D. in the UC Berkeley NLP Group, advised by Dan Klein, and my B.Sc. in the University of Sydney Schwa Lab, advised by James Curran.
